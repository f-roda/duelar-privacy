Privacy Policy for Duelar

Effective date: 2026-05-13

Duelar respects your privacy.

Information Storage

Duelar stores your journal entries, progress data, preferences, and emotional tracking information locally on your device.

Duelar does not collect, transmit, upload, or share your personal journal content with external servers.

No Account Required

Duelar does not require account creation, login, or personal identification.

Notifications

If enabled by the user, Duelar may schedule local device notifications as reminders. These notifications are generated locally on the device.

Third-Party Services

Duelar does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, cloud storage, or third-party data collection services.

Data Deletion

Because your data is stored locally on your device, uninstalling the application may permanently remove your stored content.

Medical Disclaimer

Duelar is intended as an emotional support and reflection tool only. It is not medical, psychological, or psychiatric treatment, diagnosis, or professional care.
